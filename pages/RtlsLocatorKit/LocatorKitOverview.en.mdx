# Locator Kit Overview

Congratulations on your purchase of RTLS Locator Kit. 

This section explains how to get started with RTLS Locator Kit unit. It'll direct you through the deployment rules, physical establishment, program establishment, and the initial setup to utilize Locus RTLS indoor localization system quickly and effectively. We are also ready to help you by answering your questions if you face any issues during the installation process, as a part of the implementation support that comes with your purchase – please contact our [support](/ProjectExecution/Troubleshooting/RaiseSupportTicket) in such a case.
