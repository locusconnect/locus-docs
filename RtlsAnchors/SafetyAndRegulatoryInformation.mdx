# Safety and Regulatory Information

## Safety Precautions

### Installation Safety

* Always disconnect power before installation or maintenance
* Use appropriate safety equipment when mounting anchors at height
* Secure all mounting hardware to prevent falling objects
* Follow local building codes and regulations during installation

### Electrical Safety

* Only use approved PoE power sources compliant with IEEE 802.3af
* Do not expose the anchor to water or excessive moisture
* Do not attempt to open the anchor enclosure
* Ensure proper grounding of network equipment

### Radio Frequency Safety

* The UWB radio in Locus anchors operates at very low power levels
* RF emissions are well below international safety limits
* Maintain minimum 20cm separation distance during operation
* No special precautions are required for normal use
